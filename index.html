Gang Rules

1. Code of Conduct: - All members must follow the gang's code of conduct at all times. - Respect other members and players in the game, even if they are not part of the gang..

2. Loyalty and Commitment: - Members are expected to remain loyal to the gang and prioritize its activities. - Avoid double-crossing or betraying fellow gang members.

3. Role-Playing: - Engage in role-playing activities to immerse yourself in the gang activities.

4. In-Game Behavior: - No excessive or unnecessary killing of other players. - Do not engage in griefing or trolling behavior. - Refrain from cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics.

5. Communication: - Maintain clear communication with fellow gang members, especially during fights or activities. - Use in-game chat for coordination.

6. Gang Activities: - Participate in planned gang activities such as official fights , street fights , robbery etc.. - Contribute resources or funds to support gang objectives when required.

7. Territorial Respect: - Respect the territorial boundaries of other gangs and avoid unnecessary conflict. - Defend your gang's turf when challenged but do not provoke conflicts unnecessarily.

8. Leadership and Hierarchy: - Acknowledge and follow the leadership structure of the gang. - Every suggestion is take into consideration before implemeting .

9. Recruitment : - Recruitment is done in basis of Behavior towards others and fellow mates.

10. Conflict Resolution: - Resolve conflicts or disputes among gang members privately and calmly. - Seek mediation from gang leaders if necessary.

11. Real-Life Priorities: - Real-life responsibilities and priorities come first. Do not let in-game activities negatively impact your personal life.

12. Respect the Server Rules: - Adhere to the server's rules and guidelines, in addition to the gang's rules.

13. Consistency and Activity: - Maintain consistent activity within the gang.

14. Be Inclusive: - Promote an inclusive environment that welcomes members from diverse backgrounds and experiences.
